Frequently Asked Questions

  • Why Gradify?

    Our app is crafted to make your test preparation both effective and stress-free. With realistic practice tests that mimic real-life exam conditions, you'll build the confidence needed to excel in your final exams. Plus, the app's intuitive interface and progress tracking tools simplify your study process, making it more enjoyable and keeping you motivated to achieve your best

  • Can Other Users See My Results?

    Gradify ensures your privacy by restricting access to your test scores to only the teacher who assigned the test

  • Does the App Take Up Much Space on My Phone?

    Our app is optimized to be very lightweight, ensuring that anyone can install it on their mobile device without any problems

  • How Can I Access Tests in the App?

    You can charge your points balance in the app by scanning codes available at authorized sales centers in your city. These points can be used to purchase any test uploaded by teachers within the app, with each point equivalent to one dollar
